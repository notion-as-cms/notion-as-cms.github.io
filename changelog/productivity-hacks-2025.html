    5 Productivity Hacks for 2025

    Want to get more done? Try these hacks:

    Hack #1: Time Blocking

    "Time is your most valuable asset." — Unknown
    Schedule tasks in chunks to stay focused.

    Steps to Start:

    • Pick a tool (Google Calendar, Notion).
    • Block 1-hour slots for key tasks.
    • Stick to the plan.

    Hack #2: Digital Detox

    • Turn off notifications.
    • Set phone to Do Not Disturb.
    • Check emails twice daily.
    Pro Tip: Use apps like Freedom to block distractions.
